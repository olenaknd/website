OUR WORK PACE

Applying Agile principles we, in the meanwhile, like to closely interrelate with the clients' processes for achieving maximum results.

  • 1

    REQUIREMENTS & RESEARCH

    Upon acquaintance with us the clients reveal their needs and we sign a NDA. This allows us to communicate freely for tackling the challenges and shaping the possible solutions.

  • 2

    FEATURES & ESTIMATIONS

    Proper stack's selection, definition of the project's stages and lists of assignments lead to the product's backlog and business proposal.

  • 3

    AGREEMENT

    Signing the software development agreement gives the start of a new project.

  • 4

    PLANNING

    During the meeting with a client called "sprint planning" our team selects a number of product backlog items, usually in the form of user stories.

  • 5

    DEVELOPMENT

    Our development works include specifying, designing, coding, testing, bug fixing and modifying the product thus at the end of the sprint appears the demo.

  • 6

    DELIVERY

    The successful product is delivered, validated and launched at the client's environment. Our follow up team ensures the post-release activities to assist at any time.
